Theme Tricks of the trade

To modify this theme there are some fairly simple and quicktricks:

  1. Change the images -- this will have a significant impact on the look and feel of the site
  2. Modify the style sheet colors to coordinate with your image modifications.
  3. Because this theme does not use tables, all positioning is accomplished in the style.css
  4. Be careful to follow style sheet precedence! IE and other browsers do not handle precedence and inheritance the same way. No surprise there, I guess!
